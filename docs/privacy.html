隐私政策

引言

Project 50（"我们"、"我们的"或"本应用"）尊重并保护所有使用服务用户的个人隐私权。本隐私政策将帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

信息收集

本应用不收集任何个人身份信息。我们仅在设备本地存储以下数据：

  • 您的任务设置和完成状态
  • 您的备忘录内容
  • 应用的使用偏好设置

数据存储

所有数据均存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器或与第三方共享。

数据安全

我们使用 iOS 系统提供的标准安全机制来保护您的数据。您的数据受到设备级加密的保护。

隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。当我们进行更改时，我们会在应用中通知您。

联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 App Store 中提供的开发者联系方式与我们联系。

Privacy Policy

Introduction

Project 50 ("we," "our," or "the app") respects and protects the privacy of all service users. This Privacy Policy helps you understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information Collection

This app does not collect any personal identification information. We only store the following data locally on your device:

  • Your task settings and completion status
  • Your memo contents
  • App usage preferences

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any servers or shared with third parties.

Data Security

We use standard iOS system security mechanisms to protect your data. Your data is protected by device-level encryption.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will notify you through the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the developer contact information provided in the App Store.
